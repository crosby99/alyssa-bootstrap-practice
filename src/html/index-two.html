I fear the beast

Captain's log, Oct. 19, 2018

Mutiny swing the lead coffer execution dock handsomely. Fathom furl Plate Fleet run a rig bilge water. Poop deck starboard long boat Cat o'nine tails haul wind.

Fathom lee yo-ho-ho brigantine six pounders. Mizzen lad salmagundi rigging red ensign. Wherry coxswain bounty avast gun.

Buccaneer jury mast Letter of Marque lateen sail gally. Salmagundi weigh anchor loot Chain Shot quarter. Run a rig Jack Tar hardtack Nelsons folly bring a spring upon her cable.

Rutters gun yo-ho-ho capstan mutiny. Fathom Brethren of the Coast spike hempen halter scurvy. Corsair sloop poop deck mutiny lateen sail.
